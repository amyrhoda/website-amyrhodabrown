Packages

Coaching works best when coach and coachee establish an ongoing commitment to work together, so my coaching is available through multi-session packages:

  • Career level up for tech
  • Leadership level up for tech
  • Business level up for artists
  • Custom level up for you

Career Level Up for Tech

The Career Level Up for Tech package is for you if you're a developer, engineer, and other individual contributor who:

  • wants to improve hard or soft skills
  • is in a new position, company, or culture
  • is dealing with imposter syndrome, self doubt, or an inner critic
  • is navigating the tech industry as member of a marginalized community

In our work together, you'll gain a deeper understanding of yourself, your strengths and values, and the beliefs and mindsets you hold which either support you or hold you back. You'll figure out how to leverage your strengths and assets to thrive, not just at work but in everything you do. I'll provide accountability as you commit to making changes in your behaviour and your beliefs.

Duration: 12 sessions. We'll start with a 1.5 hour discovery session, a deep dive into you and your life. For the next 11 sessions, we'll meet every other week for an hour to debrief, talk, brainstorm, envision and implement the next step in your career level up.

Also available: Career Level Up for Tech, Mini

Coaching can be a daunting expense, but I believe it's valuable to everyone, not just executives and senior leaders. So to make coaching accessible to more people, I offer a mini version of the Career Level Up. You'll get the same 1.5 hour discovery session, and then 5 more sessions, every other week, where we'll clarify your values and strengths and start to gain clarity on what's getting in your way.

If you want to talk about whether the Career Level Up is right for you, get in touch and let's chat.

Leadership Level Up for Tech

The Leadership Level Up for Tech package is for you if you're a new or seasoned leader in the tech industry:

  • starting a new role
  • leading a new team
  • facing a new challenge like remote or cross-cultural leadership
  • developing skills around trust, communication, and conflict management

In our work together you'll gain clarity on your values and strengths and how they intersect with the values and needs of your organization. You'll improve your emotional intelligence, and understand your own challenges better. I will be an impartial sounding board, a weekly oasis of quiet, to think through challenges and be held accountable as you change and learn.

Duration: 12 sessions. We'll start with a 1.5 hour discovery session, a deep dive into you, your values and your strengths. Every two week for the next 11 sessions we'll meet for an hour to talk, brainstorm, experiment, and grow your understanding and skills.

If you want to talk about whether the Leadership Level Up is right for you, get in touch and let's chat.

Business Level Up for Artists

The Business Level Up for Artists package is for you if you're a creative entrepreneur, freelance artist or performer:

  • creating a new business or product
  • expanding an existing business
  • facing imposter syndrome, self-doubt, the inner critic, or issues with procrastination, time management, or productivity

Through our work together you'll gain clarity about your values and strengths, increase your confidence in your talents and offerings, and learn how to navigate challenges like social media authenticity and vulnerability, balance and self-care, time management and organization. Our meetings will give you a quiet space in which to think out loud and explore ideas and emotions. I'll be a non-judgemental sounding board and I'll hold you accountable for the changes you commit to.

Duration: 12 sessions. We'll start with a 1.5 hour discovery session, a deep dive into you, your values and your strengths. Then every other week for the next 11 sessions we'll meet for an hour to talk, brainstorm, experiment, and grow your knowledge, courage, and skills.

If you want to talk about whether the Business Level Up is right for you, get in touch and let's chat.
