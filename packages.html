Career Level-Up for Tech

The Career Level-Up for Tech package is for you if you're a developer, engineer, and other individual contributor who wants to improve hard or soft skills, or who is navigating a new position, company, or culture. It's for you if you're dealing with imposter syndrome, self doubt, or the inner critic, or if you're navigating the tech industry as a marginalized person.

Through coaching with me, you'll gain a deeper understanding of yourself, your strengths and values, and the beliefs and mindsets you hold which either support you or hold you back. You'll figure out how to leverage your strengths and assets to thrive, not just at work but in everything you do. I'll provide accountability as you commit to making changes in your behaviour and your beliefs.

Duration: 12 sessions
We'll start with a 1.5 hour discovery session, a deep dive into you and your life. Every week for the next 11 weeks we'll meet for an hour to talk, brainstorm, envision and implement the next step in your career.

Leadership Level-Up for Tech

The Leadership Level-Up for Tech package is for you if you're a new or seasoned leader in the tech industry:

  • starting a new role
  • leading a new team
  • facing a new challenge like remote or cross-cultural leadership
  • developing skills in trust, communication, and conflict management

Through coaching with me you'll gain clarity on your own values and strengths and how they intersect with the values and needs of your organization. You'll improve your emotional intelligence, and understand your own needs and challenges better. I will be an impartial sounding board, a weekly oasis of quiet, to think through challenges and be held accountable as you change and learn.

Duration: 12 sessions
We'll start with a 1.5 hour discovery session, a deep dive into you, your values and your strengths. Every week for the next 11 weeks we'll meet for an hour to talk, brainstorm, experiment, and grow your understanding and skills.

Business Level-Up for Artists

The Business Level-Up for Artists package is for you if you're a creative entrepreneur or freelance artist or performer,

  • creating a new business or product
  • expanding an existing business
  • facing imposter syndrome, self-doubt, the inner critic, or procrastination

Through coaching with me you'll gain clarity about your values and strengths. You'll develop confidence in your talents and offerings, and learn how to navigate challenges like authenticity and vulnerability, balance and self-care, time management and organization. Our meetings will give you a quiet space in which to think out loud and explore ideas and emotions. I'll be a non-judgemental sounding board and I'll hold you accountable for the changes you choose to make.

Duration: 12 sessions
We'll start with a 1.5 hour discovery session, a deep dive into you, your values and your strengths. Every week for the next 11 weeks we'll meet for an hour to talk, brainstorm, experiment, and grow your understanding and skills.
