Personal Development Coach

Coaching is about change; change in what you know, what you do, or how you feel about your life.

As an Adler-trained coach, I offer the gifts of attentive listening (when is that last time someone really listened to you), insightful questions, and the Beginner's Mind.

Most importantly, I offer trust in you: trust that you know what you need, that you know what to do, and that you can achieve your goals. (Yes — even if you don't trust yourself.)

Together we will discover what you need to change (if you don't already know), and figure out your next move. Then I will support you (and hold you accountable) while you take those steps to becoming your unique Best Self.
