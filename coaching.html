How Does Coaching Work?

Coaching is an ongoing process which takes place over several weeks. I offer coaching in six- to ten-session packages, or as an ongoing relationship.

The first meeting of our series of sessions will be pretty long: ninety minutes to two hours. We'll talk about what is on your mind, what your goals are, what you would like to change or work on. We'll get into who you are, what your values are, whether you feel like you have balance in your life, and whatever other topics are appropriate in order to help you move forward.

After the first meeting, we'll meet every two weeks for an hour to see how you're doing and to dig further into our conversations about values, choices, and everything else.

If you are interested in coaching — or even just curious — contact me to arrange a twenty-minute phone consultation, with no charge and no commitment.
