Editing

I offer all levels of editing for academic, scientific, and and non-fiction writing, including grant proposals, theses, letters, articles and books. I edit for professional, academic, and lay audiences.

Although I work with all kinds of science writing, I focus on psychology, sociology, climate change, and computer science writing. I also have a particular interest in editing works about First Nations issues.

I have experience editing grant proposals, technical articles, papers, letters, books, and other technical and academic writing. I enjoy working with writers who are non-native English speakers, and writers who are self-publishing.
