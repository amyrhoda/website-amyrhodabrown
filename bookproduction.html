Book Production for Self-Publishers

There has never been a better time to get a book into print. With the rise in self-publishing and print-on-demand services, you can publish a top-quality edition of your book independently, without running a gauntlet of agents and publishing house gatekeepers.

What separates high quality self-published books from "vanity press" books is attention to detail and knowledge of how books are produced. A project editor can oversee the entire production process, from editing and typesetting to proofreading and cover design, as well as the hassles of dealing with the print-on-demand services.

For more information about what is required to produce a good self-published book, read my article "How to Make a Book".
