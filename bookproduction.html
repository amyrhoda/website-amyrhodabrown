Editing and Custom Book Production

Book Production for Self-Publishers

If you have written a book, there has never been a better time to get it into print. With the rise in self-publishing and print-on-demand services, it's possible to publish a top quality edition of your book independently, without running the gauntlet of agents and publishing house gatekeepers.

What separates a high quality self-published book from the "vanity press" books cluttering the market is the attention to detail that a professional project editor can bring. I will not only edit your manuscript at the level you require, I will design and lay out the book to be professional, appealing and readable, create an eye-catching cover, and help you get it into print through the publishing and distribution service of your choice.

Whether it's non-fiction, a memoir, a collection of poems, or a novel, I can help you bring your work to the market. I offer a full range of pre-publication services, individually or as a package:

  • developmental editing
  • copyediting
  • typesetting for self-publishing
  • book and cover design for self-publishing
  • support through the self-publishing process

Contact me to discuss your project; I am happy to help you determine the amount and level of editing and publishing assistance you need.
