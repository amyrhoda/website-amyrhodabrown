Book Production for Self-Publishers

There has never been a better time to get a book into print. With the rise in self-publishing and print-on-demand services, you can publish a top-quality edition of your book independently, without committing to a large print-run or having to go through a traditional publishing house.

What separates high quality self-published books from the others is attention to detail and knowledge of how books are produced. A project editor oversees the entire production process, from editing and typesetting to proofreading and cover design, as well as dealing with the print-on-demand, ebook production, and distribution services.

(For more information about what is required to produce a good self-published book, read my article "How to Make a Book".)

In addition to full project editing, I offer à la carte:

  • editing at any level
  • book design and typesetting
  • cover design
  • proofreading
