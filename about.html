About Amy

I love to read, and I love books, not just because they contain lots of words to read, but as objects in their own right. I like the way they feel and look, and I like all the decisions that go into making a book: how big should it be, what goes on the cover, what typefaces should we use?

I'm a member of the Editors Association of Canada, and I often attend their excellent seminars. I also improve my editing skills and knowledge by reading books, or simply consulting with other editors.

I also love science: the process of it and the truths that it reveals. I like knowing new things, but I also enjoy that state of not knowing, when we have some hypotheses but we're not sure which one is right. I also like that feeling when you find out that something you thought was true isn't; I like to be suprised and challenged.

I live in Toronto with my husband and two daughters. When I'm not helping people communicate, I read (for fun), sing, walk, and hang out with my kids.
