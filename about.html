About Amy

I love to read, and I love books &emdash; not just because they contain lots of words to read, but as objects in their own right. I like the way they feel and look.

I'm a member of the Editors Association of Canada, and I attend their excellent seminars as often as I can. I also improve my editing skills and knowledge by reading books, and consulting with other editors.

I live in Toronto with my husband and two daughters. When I'm not helping people communicate, I read (for fun), sing, walk, write, and hang out with my kids.
